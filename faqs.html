ORDERS & SHIPPING

Orders are shipped Monday to Friday between the hours of 9AM and 5PM GMT. This excludes weekend days and UK Bank Holidays. Our team will process your order within 48 hours, we then hand it over to our nominated carriers.

We track every order. We'll send you the tracking number of your order and you can track it here. If you have any issue with your tracking information, email us at support@lluxbeauty.com and we will be happy to help.

Unfortunately we start processing your order as soon as it's placed. We are unable to change or cancel orders once they are made. if you made an error at checkout, please reach out to support@lluxbeauty.com.

Unfortunately, we are unable to make any changes to your order once you have placed this however if you email us at support@lluxbeauty.com with your order number, one of our team will be able to advise the best next steps to take.

We’re so sorry to hear that you were sent the incorrect item. We’d love to sort this for you! Please email us on support@lluxbeauty.com with your order number and a photo of what you have received. One of our team will be happy to help!

INTERNATIONAL ORDERS

We ship worldwide. Although we may not ship to some countries due to their cross-border policy requirements. This includes Russia, Barbados, St Martin, Kazakhstan, Belarus, Martinique, Yemen, Libya, Tanzania, Argentina and Mexico.

We cover all tax and duties to EU & UAE. For U.S orders the duties will be calculated at checkout. For any other countries outside of the United Kingdom you may be subject to import duties and taxes, which are issued once the package reaches your country. Customs policies vary from country to country, so you should contact your local customs office for further information.
