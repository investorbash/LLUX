PRIVACY POLICY

What Is Privacy Policy

This privacy policy gives you information on how L'LUX BEAUTY collects personal data through your use of our website, including any data you may provide through this website when you purchase a product, sign up to receive marketing information from us, take part in a competition, use an application or submit an event enquiry form. The data we collect is used to receive your order, process your payment and send your purchase to you. We may also deliver marketing communications, personalise your visit to our site and provide assistance via our support teams. If you have any queries on this privacy policy or any questions about exercising your rights in respect of your personal data we hold or handle then please contact us on support@lluxbeauty.com We must have a valid cause to use your data. There are four primary reasons for use of your data: contractual, legal obligation, consent which you provide to us & our legitimate business interests. Generally, we do not rely on consent as a legal basis for processing your personal data although we will get your consent before sending third party direct marketing communications to you via email or text message. You have the right to withdraw consent to marketing at any time by contacting us. You don’t have to give us any of this personal information but if you don’t, you may not be able to purchase our products via the website, and you are unlikely to be able to receive the customer service that we aim to provide. We also anonymise and aggregate personal information (so that it does not identify you) and use it for purposes including testing our IT systems, research, data analysis, improving our website, and developing new products. We also share this information with third parties. We will only use your personal data when the law allows us to.

Data Controller

L'LUX BEAUTY LTD is the data controller (i.e. the company who collects your data) and we control how it is and will be used. We are responsible for holding your data is securely & ensuring that your rights as a user are respected. We are committed to protecting the personal data of our visitors. We do not market our products, nor do we aim an individual product, at anyone who is under 18. We cannot identify individuals who are 18 or under within our database.

Data Sharing

We do not, and will not, sell any of your personal data to any third party. We may from time to time share your data with the following categories of companies as an essential part of being able to provide our services to you:

  • Companies that help us complete your order, such as payment service providers, warehouses, and delivery companies; and
  • Legal advisers, bankers, auditors and insurers and our regulators, including HM Revenue & Customs and with any third parties to who we choose to sell or transfer any part of our business or assets.
Data Security

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used or accessed in an unauthorised way, altered or disclosed. We also limit access to your personal data to those employees, agents, contractors and other third parties who have a business need to know. They will only process your personal data on L'LUX Beauty’s instructions and they are subject to a duty of confidentiality. We have put in place appropriate procedures to deal with any suspected personal data breach and will notify you and any applicable regulator of a breach where we are legally required to do so.
