RETURN AND REFUND POLICY

We're sorry if you didn't love your product from L'LUX!

To request a return please visit our return portal here: https://refybeauty.com/a/returns. You will need your email and order number to process this request.

For full information on our refund policy, please read below:

  • We have a 14 day refund policy; from the day the item is ordered, you will have 14 days to request a return.
  • We only offer refunds if product packaging is unopened, the product is unused, the packaging is in a re-sellable condition and the refund request is made within 14 days of order. This is for hygiene reasons.
  • Please note, that we do not cover the cost of postage to return to us.
  • If you are returning items to us, we do advise that you track your return as we are not liable for any parcels lost within the postal service.
  • Please note that for Collection purchases, individual items cannot be returned separately.
  • If your order contained a free promotional gift, the gift must be included with the return of any related orders/items.
  • If you have received a different item from the one you ordered - we're sorry! We will get the right product to you - just email support@lluxbeauty.com with the order details and we will sort the rest!

Unfortunately, any items that are not returned as per the above guidelines won't be eligible for a refund. For any additional information, please email support@lluxbeauty.com
